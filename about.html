FRONTEND DEVELOPER JUNIOR STUDENT

Hi, my name is Ida Casperson, and I am 27 years old. I live in Mölndal which is located just next to Gothenburg. I am a person who enjoys spending time with friends and family in my free time and whenever possible I like to engage in CrossFit training. Sailing along the west coast during the summers is a significant passion of mine. Traveling or going on a spontaneous road trip is also something I never say no to.

I am currently in my first year of studying Frontend Development at the Media Institute in Gothenburg. At the same time I work weekend shifts at Volvo Cars in Torslanda, where I ensure that the cars being built are watertight and soundproof. I prepare the car bodies for various chemical baths before they move on to the assembly factory. I have been in Volvo Cars for almost 7 years now and have been fortunate to work in various roles primarily within logistics. In recent years I have been in the paint shop but now I feel the need to continue my development, which led me to choose the Frontend Developer program.

At the moment I am in the process of learning HTML and CSS, and very soon, I will be diving into JAVASCRIPT as well.
